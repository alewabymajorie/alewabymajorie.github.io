About Us

Welcome To AlewaBy Majorie

The Alewa by Majorie brand gives you a feeling of belonging and a shopping experience that excites you as every piece you buy is handmade by experienced locals with raw materials sourced in Ghana and all over Africa.

You will enjoy value as we work on giving you exceptional but affordable products and a good ready to serve customer service. 

VIEW OUR PRODUCTS

THE STORY
The global growth of the fashion industry in the past years influenced the birth of the brand Alewa by Majorie, founded by Majorie Martey from Ghana. The brand aims to tell the African Fashion story the African way by Africans by blending vintage African style with modern trends. Be ready to feel the culture as we bridge the gap in the industry for affordable and colorful contemporary African pieces that will awaken your love for African print and Fashion.
